Dangers of polynomial fitting¶

Be careful with polynomial curves: they tend to overfit the data. That is, the curve will appear to follow very closely your data, but it will have erratic/unexpected results for large or small $x$-values (outside your data domain, to be more precise.)

For example, change x_pred = np.linspace(x.min(), x.max()) to x_pred = np.linspace(300, 500). Or what about from 100 to 700? How does the curve look now? Would you trust the curve?
